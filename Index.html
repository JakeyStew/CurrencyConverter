Welcome to the currency converter. Here we have a simple web application that allows you to price a basket of many goods in a number of different currencies.

The tool is very simple to use, just add or remove any items you like via the “+” and “-“ buttons on an item slide. As you increase or decrease the total amount of items the grand total will update accordingly. You may also change the currency type using the drop down menu below, this will update all amounts for the currency you have chosen instantly.
